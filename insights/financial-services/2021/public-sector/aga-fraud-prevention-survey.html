AGA Fraud Prevention Survey

By Caitlin McGurn

April 16, 2021
April 16, 2021
AGA Fraud Prevention Survey

While many agencies remain confident in their ability to proactively address fraud prevention and detection, fraud remains a moving and elusive target, with the pandemic introducing a slew of unforeseen challenges and risks.

To that end, AGA teamed with our Corporate Partner Alpha Genesisto offer government agencies the opportunity to participate in a new survey on trends in fraud prevention and detection. The outcome will help determine how government agencies are responding to new and escalating fraud challenges as a collective group.

We encourage you to participate in this survey to help agencies like yours uncover and address:

  • Trends in fraud prevention and detection.
  • Types of fraud that expose your organization to the greatest risk for loss.
  • Best practices and technologies implemented to detect and prevent fraud.

We designed this survey with your busy schedule in mind and it should only take approximately 10 minutes to complete. 

We will share the results of this survey at AGA’s Professional Development Training in July. The report will also be shared with AGA members and posted to the website. 

Thank you for your time and input — all of which will help us to help you!
