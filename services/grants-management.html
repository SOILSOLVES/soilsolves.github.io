Grants Management

Grants Management
Overview Industries Case Studies Insights Experts Connect

Our grants management services bridge the gap between grantor and recipient. We work across industries with federal agencies, state and local organizations, and grant recipients to help align requirements and expectations on all sides.

Using our integrated program management office, TruePMOSM, we help organizations streamline ways of working to enhance the impact of grant funding more efficiently.

Industry Impact

Defense & Security

From pre-award through closeout, we help agencies develop effective strategies throughout the grant’s life cycle to improve successful oversight and execution of grant funds.

Energy, Sustainability & Infrastructure

Our expertise in federal funding programs spans agencies using an approach that includes full lifecycle grants preparation and administration frameworks that ease operationalization and compliance.

Financial Services

We address compliance, due diligence, governance, internal controls, oversight, process design, program management, reporting, and technical assistance for the largest financial assistance programs in federal government.

Health

Our grants experts use their extensive experience to help grants stakeholders reduce burdens, improve transparency, standardize processes, manage risk, and measure impact.

State & Local Government

We help state and local governments develop a more organized and efficient process for preparing, managing, and reporting grants.
