Supply Chain

Supply Chain
Overview Industries Case Studies Insights Experts Connect

Maintaining the integrity of the supply chain and logistics operations while evolving to address new sustainability, security and resiliency demands requires a different approach with modern solutions to navigate forward. We offer a comprehensive suite of digital services that enable public and commercial sector organizations to confidently reduce risks, improve profitability, and leverage intelligence along the way. Our team uses global, industry-standard measurement frameworks to guide clients and recommend improvements to end-to-end supply chain operations to achieve their sustainability goals.

Industries Supported

Defense & Security

We integrate strategy, risk management, supply chain security, and performance management to support supply chain management programs from design and inception through their full life-cycle to operational maturity.

Energy, Sustainability & Infrastructure

To help their critical role in helping brands reach decarbonization targets, we developed the Supplier LoCT collaborative to help suppliers reduce GHG emissions.

Health

Our healthcare experts help organizations build or enhance their supply chain capabilities, without the conflicts of interest with manufacturers, group purchasing organizations, and distributor-owned consultancies.
