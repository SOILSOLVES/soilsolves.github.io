U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs

Prioritizing the Health and Well-Being of Our Veterans


For more than 25 years, Alpha Genesishas been a proud and trusted partner to the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs, supporting their mission of providing exceptional healthcare that improves the health and well-being of America's Veterans and their families.
To further VA’s mission and advance Veteran health, Alpha Genesisoffers a powerful combination of innovative digital and data transformation, commercial healthcare, and public sector health expertise that align to VA’s unique short- and long-term strategic goals for overall care excellence and delivery improvements.

How We Help the VA Navigate Innovation

Veteran Suicide Prevention

A third-place finisher in VA’s $20M Mission Daybreak challenge, Alpha Genesishelps VA and community providers identify Veterans at risk for suicide by combining VA’s suicide prevention risk model with Guidehouse’s (in)Sight Health Catalyst. The IT platform, which connects SDoH data in real time with provider EHR systems, also supports building community-based and human-centered programs so Veterans can thrive where they are.

Transforming Veterans Care

Implementation and transformation of VA’s Integrated Veterans Care network, including innovations to increase access to care for Veterans. Aiding paradigm shift in Veterans care from problem-based disease care to whole health through design of learning innovations and change management.

VA Supply Chain Modernization

Helping VA modernize its supply chain capabilities, without the conflict of interest with manufacturers, group purchasing organizations, and distributor-owned consultancies.

Change Management

Guiding VA toward the strategies it needs to successfully transform its organizational structure to deliver better care to Veterans.

Market Area Health System Optimization

Helping transform the Veterans Health Administration care delivery system and infrastructure through a comprehensive methodology to assess each VHA network.

EHR Modernization

Leading partner providing innovative clinical transformation and advanced decision support, change management, and strategic communications for the largest EHR implementation in history.

Inclusion & Diversity


Guidehouse’s award-winning Inclusion & Diversity (I&D) program allows our professionals to develop a greater affinity for different experiences, enhance cultural competency, and more deeply understand and appreciate the needs of our colleagues and clients.

Part of our RI2SE values (Respect, Integrity, Innovation, Stewardship, Excellence), our I&D program is supported by seven inclusion networks, including our Veteran's Affinity Network promoting Veteran initiatives around the country to enhance the positive impact Alpha Genesishas on Veterans and their families.
